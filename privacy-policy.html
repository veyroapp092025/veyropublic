Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 23, 2025

Important Notice: Veyro currently uses sample/demo data to demonstrate how the app works. We do not currently connect to real bank accounts or access your actual financial data.

1. Introduction

Welcome to Veyro ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we handle your information. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

By using the Veyro mobile application (the "App"), you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Commitment Data: When you commit to fixing a spending habit, we store your selected category, target savings amount, and commitment start date locally on your device.
  • App Preferences: Settings and preferences you choose within the app (e.g., theme preferences).

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Analytics Data: We use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymized usage data, including:
    • App screens visited
    • Features used (e.g., viewing money leaks, committing to habits)
    • Time spent in the app
    • Device information (model, operating system, app version)
    • General location data (country/city level only)
  • Crash Reports: Diagnostic information to help us fix bugs and improve app stability.

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • ❌ Real bank account credentials
  • ❌ Actual transaction data
  • ❌ Personal identification documents
  • ❌ Payment information
  • ❌ Contact lists or phone numbers
  • ❌ Precise location data
  • ❌ Camera or microphone access

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • App Functionality: To provide core features like tracking your 30-day commitment and displaying your progress.
  • Personalization: To remember your preferences and commitment status.
  • Analytics: To understand how users interact with the app and validate our product concept.
  • Improvement: To identify bugs, improve performance, and develop new features.
  • Communication: To send important updates about the app (if we add this feature in the future).

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

Your commitment data and preferences are stored locally on your device using secure storage mechanisms provided by your operating system (Android SharedPreferences). This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to back it up through your device's system backup.

4.2 Cloud Storage (Analytics Only)

Analytics data is transmitted to Firebase (Google) servers for processing. This data is anonymized and aggregated. We cannot identify individual users from this data.

4.3 Security Measures

  • Data transmission uses industry-standard encryption (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Analytics data is anonymized before transmission
  • No sensitive financial data is collected or transmitted
  • Regular security updates and patches

5. Data Sharing and Third Parties

5.1 Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Analytics (Google): For anonymized usage analytics and crash reporting. Firebase Privacy Policy

5.2 We Do NOT Share Your Data With:

  • ❌ Advertisers or marketing companies
  • ❌ Data brokers
  • ❌ Social media platforms
  • ❌ Other financial institutions

5.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, legal process, or government request.

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 Access and Control

  • View Your Data: All your commitment data is visible within the app (Progress screen).
  • Delete Your Data: You can clear your commitment at any time from the Progress screen. Uninstalling the app deletes all locally stored data.
  • Opt-Out of Analytics: You can limit analytics collection through your device settings (Android: Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization).

6.2 Data Portability

Since all your data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over it. You can back it up using your device's system backup features.

7. Children's Privacy

Veyro is not intended for use by children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

8. International Users

Veyro is currently designed for users in India. If you use the app from other countries, please note that your analytics data may be transferred to and processed in countries where Firebase operates, including the United States.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Showing an in-app notification (for significant changes)
  • Requiring acceptance of new terms (for material changes)

Your continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Future Features

As Veyro evolves, we may add features that involve additional data collection, such as:

  • User accounts (email, profile)
  • Real bank account connections (with your explicit consent)
  • Transaction data analysis

Important: We will update this Privacy Policy and obtain your consent before implementing any such features.
